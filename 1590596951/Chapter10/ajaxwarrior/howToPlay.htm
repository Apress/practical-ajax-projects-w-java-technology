How To Play The Game

Your goal is first to collect the five keys. Three of them can be found, two of them are given to you by specific characters if you can get their karma high enough when talking to them. Try and choose replies that you think will result in an increase in their karma. You will not always be right! If a character's karma gets too low, they will run away from you.

Each key opens a specific door for each artifact. How to identify a door is up to you to figure out! It isn't as obvious as it seems! It is also up to you to determine which key goes with which door (but, you will not have to select a key... as long as you have a key in inventory, that is good enough).

Use the arrow keys to move. Note that you will not be able to reach the very edges of the maps, you will be stopped a few spaces before it.

Press the A key to switch between Attack mode (red border) and Talk mode (blue border). When in Attack mode, you will attack any character you come into contact with. When in Talk mode, you will talk to any non-belligerent character who's language you can speak.

Contact with a character triggers battle or talking (if you can speak the character's language).

Press the W key to switch weapons. You will be presented with a list of the weapons you have. Press the appropriate key shown, or spacebar to cancel.

Press the C key to cast a spell. You will be presented with a list of the spells you have. Press the appropriate key shown, or speacebar to cancel.

Press the I key to see your inventory. Press spacebar when done. This cannot be done in battle or when talking to a character.

Press the P button to pick up an item (gold, key, artifact, health or spell scroll). You must be standing on top of it.

Press E to enter a community (castle, town or village). You must be standing on it.

Press S to save your game. This cannot be done in battle or when talking to a character. To continue your game later, from the title screen, enter the name of your player, and click Continue Game. Note that the name must match exactly what you entered when you started the game (except for case, which will be ignores).

Press H to see a help screen.

When in battle, press F to fire a projectile weapon. You must have previously switched to a projectil weapon (slingshot or crossbow).

To quit the game, simply close the window. However, note that your progress IS NOT automatically saved! If you do not save first, too bad for you!

Note that whenever battle or a conersation with a character ends, that character is randomly placed elsewhere on the map, so you will not be able to immediately engage them again.

In each of the two towns you will find a store. To enter the store, simply step onto the tiles directly in front of it. You will be presented with a menu where you can select items to purchase, if you have enough gold of course! Press spacebar to exit the store.

One word of caution: it is possible to kill the keymasters before they have given you their key, in which case the game is unwinnable. More importantly, Mallizant has booby-trapped them to explode with magical force when killed (if they are still in posession of the key), which means that if they go, you go! So, don't attack any being unless you are sure they are not a keymaster! You can of course kill them after they give you their key, but that's just wrong, you sick person you!
